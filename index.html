Welcome to me...

Creating things. It's what I love to do. Taking my ideas from paper and pen (and sometimes crayon) and making them come to life. Either through canvus and paint or in a computer program in the form of a brochure, logo, ad, or just a cartoon. I started creating with paint when I was 7 years old. That first brush stroke in a basement on the beach has lead me here to graphic and web design. I am a bit new to web design and programming, and you will find I am a quick study. Within these pages you will find me. My style and, hopefully, my voice. Please to enjoy.
